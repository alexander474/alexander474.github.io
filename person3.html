HISTORY

After almost 100 years in human captivity The Greys broke free. They managed to escape from Area 51 and take shelter somewhere in the Nevada desert. The humans tried to find their hiding place but with no luck. After some years the humans concluded that they had left the planet. As we know now, they were wrong! The Grays were hiding deep below the ground for years. Their plan was to hide until they had a large enough army to take over the earth. Their number were up to 100 million just within a few years because of their astonishing reproduction rate. It took no more than months before the whole human species was under the commandment of The Greys and they dominated the earth for 28 years.
