STALIN

HISTORY

Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin was a Georgian-born Soviet revolutionary and political leader. He was the dictator for the Soviet Union from the mid-1920s until his death in 1953. Born into a poor family in Gori, Russia, as a youth Stalin joined the Marxist Russian Social Democratic Labour Party. In the years following the death of Vladimir Lenin in 1924, Stalin rose to become the leader of the Soviet Union.
